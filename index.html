Crystal Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of each game.

By clicking on one of the four crystals below, you will add different amounts of points to your total score.

If you match your total score to the randome number given you win! If your total exceeds the randome number you lose!

The value of each crystal is hidden.

At the start of each game the value resets.
